05 // reflections

Creating my electronic portfolio for the subject Living in the IT Era made me realize how much technology has become part of who I am as a student and as a person. At first, I thought it would just be a simple requirement where I compile my outputs, but as I worked on it, I saw it as a reflection of my growth, skills, and experiences. Choosing what to include wasn’t always easy because I wanted it to truly represent me not just my academic work, but also my effort, creativity, and progress. I became more aware of how I present myself online, knowing that an e -portfolio can also serve as a digital identity that others may see in the future. Through this process, I also developed a deeper appreciation for technology and how it helps us express ourselves in more meaningful ways. I learned not just technical skills, but also the importance of organization, creativity, and authenticity. It challenged me to be more thoughtful about my work and how I communicate my ideas. In the end, creating my e-portfolio wasn’t just about completing a task it was about understanding my journey in this digital age and preparing myself for future opportunities where technology plays a big role.
