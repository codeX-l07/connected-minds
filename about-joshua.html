01 // Daily communication

In my daily routine,

I mostly communicate through online platforms because of my school setup. I use Messenger to chat with my classmates and teachers, especially when we have updates, group works, or questions about our lessons. It’s very helpful because I can easily send messages and get quick responses anytime. For our classes and meetings, I use Google Meet where I join discussions and listen to lectures. Even though it’s not face-to-face, I still try to participate and express my ideas clearly. Sometimes there are challenges like internet connection, but I do my best to stay present and active. These platforms really help me stay connected and updated with everything in school. Overall, my daily communication shows how I adjust and stay responsible in an online learning environment.

02 // hobbies & talents

My talent as a local rapper comes from my desire to tell real stories through music. I write and perform songs that reflect the struggles people face every day, especially the challenges that often go unnoticed. Through my lyrics, I try to paint a clear picture of reality both the pain and the hope that exist in our communities. I use rap as my voice to express emotions that are sometimes hard to say directly. My music also talks about the situation of our nation, including the effects of a cruel and unjust government. I want listeners to feel the truth in every word and realize that these issues are real and happening now. At the same time, I aim to inspire others to stay strong and aware despite these hardships. Writing songs is not just a hobby for me, it’s a way to connect, inform, and stand up for what is right. Through my talent, I hope to make people think, feel, and take action in their own way.
