05 // reflections

Creating our e-portfolio was an eye-opening experience that taught us valuable lessons about technology, design, and academic integrity. Using tools like Wix.com allowed us to build a website without coding, which helped me and us experiment with layouts and user experience. Through this process, I learned the importance of original content and how to avoid plagiarism by properly citing sources. Organizing information with hyperlinks improved navigation, while focusing on visual consistency in colors and fonts strengthened the overall design. This project highlighted the balance between creativity and functionality in digital work.
